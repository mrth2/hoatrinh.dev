A Turborepo hosting a Win95-themed game hub plus individual games — Snake, Solitaire, Minesweeper, Pinball — built on Phaser 3 and SvelteKit 5.

Players earn WXP points, climb daily/weekly/all-time leaderboards, take on monthly challenges, and unlock themes as they level up. The hub itself is Astro 5 + SvelteKit 5 on Cloudflare Pages with a D1 database and Better Auth.

A love letter to '90s desktop nostalgia with none of the install.
