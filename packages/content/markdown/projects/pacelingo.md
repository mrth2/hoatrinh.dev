A private PWA I built so my family can practice English on their own pace. Walkie-talkie voice UX with push-to-talk, speech-to-text, and TTS playback; Gemini-backed lessons for Free Talk, Pronunciation, and Vocabulary.

Auto-extracts vocabulary and common mistakes into a per-learner word bank, generates post-session summaries, and tracks streaks. Anonymous auth keeps it frictionless — no accounts, multiple profiles per device.
